Privacy Policy

At GoodiNeewz Pharmacy, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and health information.

Information We Collect

  • Name, address, phone number, and email
  • Prescription details and medical history (only when uploaded)
  • Payment and transaction information
  • IP address, browser type, and device information

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your medicine orders
  • To verify prescriptions with registered pharmacists
  • To communicate order status and updates
  • To improve our services and prevent fraud

Data Security

We use SSL encryption and comply with Indian pharmacy laws (Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940). Your prescriptions are stored securely and deleted after 2 years.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do NOT sell your data. We may share information only with:

  • Licensed pharmacists for prescription verification
  • Delivery partners (name, address, phone only)
  • Payment gateways (secure, encrypted)
